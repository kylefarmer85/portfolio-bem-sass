About

It all started when my 3rd grade teacher, Mrs. Barnes, asked me to join the computer team and teach the rest of the students how to use the school's new Macintosh computers. They were truly magical and I was hooked immediately (google "flying toasters"). Surely, this would be my calling. However, in 8th grade a different path presented itself when I got my first guitar.

Fast forward to today— after a decade in the live music industry, I’m returning to my original fascination. I bring 10 years of experience collaborating with diverse teams from all over the world, creative problem-solving while under pressure, and the work ethic to never give up— “the show must go on” rings surprisingly true.

I'm passionate about the way software looks, feels, and functions for the user, and my best work is achieved while collaborating with others.
